title: "Deceleration of Length & Precision"
---

This post is a deceleration. It is a reminder to myself that it is okay if blog posts are short and communicate one point and are not technically beautiful. They can be as short as one sentence, or as long as a book. Because at the end of the day, the goal is to communicate something, and that something has the optionality to be as long as I want. I hope to communicate ideas I have researched, or ideas I have thought about for 5 minutes; either way, it's my writing and I need to get over the fact that not every piece I put out will be perfect or beautiful or lengthy or strong. The point is to show others what I am thinking about and how that might help them.
